About TaskFlow

A minimal, powerful task manager built for real focus.

Our Mission

Built to help you focus on what matters.

TaskFlow is a lightweight, browser-based task management system designed with clarity and speed in mind. No sign-ups, no servers — your tasks live in your browser and stay private to you.

Whether you're managing work, studies, or personal goals — TaskFlow keeps it simple and powerful.

Fast & Instant

No loading screens. Tasks save instantly to your browser's localStorage.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Create Tasks

Add tasks instantly with name, description, priority level, and category tags.

Edit Anytime

Update task details on the fly without losing any of your existing data.

Track Progress

Mark tasks as complete and monitor your completion rate at a glance.

Search & Filter

Find any task instantly with live search, or filter by status.

Persistent Storage

Tasks are saved in localStorage — they survive page refreshes automatically.

Clean Design

A cool, light-blue aesthetic that's easy on the eyes and delightful to use.
